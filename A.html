Exhibition

Quiet Voltage balances softness, signal, and material tension.

The season pairs intimate painting with sculptural form and experimental digital works, keeping the show cohesive without flattening its range.

Curator's note

We built the show around contrast.

Soft light against hard edges, silence against signal, and the hand-made beside the computational. That balance gives the exhibition a high-end feel without losing warmth or accessibility.

About

A gallery experience shaped like a premium editorial product.

ArtVista presents contemporary work with the clarity collectors expect and the warmth visitors remember.

Founded in 2018, ArtVista is a contemporary art space dedicated to showcasing emerging and mid-career artists from around the globe. Our mission is to bridge the gap between creators and collectors, fostering a deeper appreciation for visual storytelling.

With three exhibition halls and a rotating calendar of avant-garde installations, we invite you to discover works that challenge, inspire, and transform. Our permanent collection includes over 200 pieces spanning painting, sculpture, photography, and new media.

Located in the heart of the Arts District — open Tuesday to Sunday.
